Frequently Asked Questions

How does UpNotify's AI-powered filtering work?

Our AI analyzes your profile, past bidding history, and job preferences to intelligently filter and rank job postings. It learns from your interactions, continuously improving its recommendations over time.

Can I still use UpNotify with my existing integrations?

Absolutely! UpNotify provides an RSS feed compatible with the deprecated Upwork format. Simply replace your old Upwork RSS URL with your new UpNotify URL, and your existing integrations will continue to work seamlessly.
